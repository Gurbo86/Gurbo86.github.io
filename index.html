About Me

Hi, my name is Leonel Maximo Maguet but I’ve been using the nickname Gurbo for a while.

I’ve been interested in games since I was a boy, playing them made me realize that I wanted to make them, so I’ve tried very hard to find a way to make a living of this industry in Argentina.

I did lots of volunteering for the past years and I made it to the final stage of a few enterpreneur competitions. I have been a Programmer, an Entrepreneur and an Investigator, also, in the past few years I´ve been working as Game Designer and I´ve been teaching Game Design classes at different institutes and universities.

At this page I intend to put together all the games I’ve been working on and also, offer my two cents about games in the form of thoughts and rant posts.

I hope you enjoy them.
