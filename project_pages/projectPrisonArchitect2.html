ABOUT PRISON ARCHITECT 2

Prison Architect 2, the ruthless prison simulator that propels the saga into a cutting-edge 3D realm. Forge multi-storey mega-fortresses of steel and concrete, lay out impossible wings and quell riots in real time thanks to deeper simulation and chillingly realistic inmate behaviour. Developed by Double Eleven and Kokku under the Paradox Interactive banner, it launches on PC, PS5 and Xbox Series X|S bearing the legacy of its predecessor: over 4 million players and 10 official expansions that cemented a true empire behind bars.

FEATURES

  • Now in 3D carring including fully dynamic cameras and lighting.
  • Vertical construction enabling multi-storey blocks, catwalks and walkways.
  • Advanced social AI with friendships and rivalries that shape behaviour.
  • Choices that can trigger breakouts, gang wars and real-time disasters.
  • New rooms, objects and tools to customise every corner of the facility.

Project Description

Prison Architect 2 is a sequel to the original Prison Architect, a game that has been a staple in the simulation genre. This new installment aims to take the franchise to new heights with its advanced 3D graphics, deeper simulation mechanics, and enhanced AI systems.

Project Contributions

During my time at Double Eleven, I contributed to various aspects of Prison Architect 2, including UI development, gameplay features, and internal tools. My work involved close collaboration with the animation, audio, and design teams. Through the development, I was able to learn new technologies, expanding my development skills, and also improved my communication abilities.

Unfortunately, the development of the game has been postponed until new confirmations are received, but as a personal experience, it was quite nourishing.
